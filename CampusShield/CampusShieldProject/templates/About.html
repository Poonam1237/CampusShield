About CampusShield
CampusShield - Anonymous Reporting Tool is a student-focused platform created to empower individuals to speak up without fear. Many hesitate to report issues such as harassment, bullying, or ragging due to fear of retaliation or lack of support.
CampusShield bridges this gap.
Why CampusShield?
Students often suffer in silence because they are afraid or unsure where to report concerns. This tool allows them to submit complaints anonymously while ensuring the reports are directed to the appropriate campus authorities.Many students hesitate to report incidents such as harassment, bullying, or misconduct due to fear of retaliation, lack of anonymity, or uncertainty about where to turn. CampusShield offers a confidential and anonymous platform where students can safely voice their concerns. By ensuring reports reach the appropriate authorities while protecting student identity, CampusShield encourages a more transparent and accountable campus environment.
